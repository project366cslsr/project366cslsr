Somewhat Garage Rock'N'Roll inspired Psychobilly, with a certain mood and twist.


"Come With Us" - perhaps sort of Psychobilly with high influence of Punky rockin' and rollin'. A bit sloppy, lightly rough vocals, but an abundance of guitar rave and excessive pounding drums rhythm, as well as fast doublebass with a very strong emphasis on self. But suddenly the transition to catchy sing-a-long and enjoying mood. And on repeat! This is generally true for the other two compositions too. "Ballroom Blitz" cover with practically its classic psycho version. Raw, screeches, squeals, wavy, rhythmically, groovin' and high energy rocking fun. "Good And Honest" - the band's furious and wild hymn. Yes, it was good and honest. Like the whole EP, fair enough with quite a garage mood.
