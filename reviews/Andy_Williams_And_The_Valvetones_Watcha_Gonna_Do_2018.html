Not a bad cover version of a fairly well-known and popular song, sounding as usual in rather theatrical and tragic tones. Not as so desperate sound as with the original, but a somewhat sorrowful and raw Rockabilly ballad. Such a bristly sound is so atmospheric. And all this profound chant turns into a story.
