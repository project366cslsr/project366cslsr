Norwegian Country music with an original attitude. Pretty and beautiful modern Country sound with melodious singing, vocals and voices. But what is much more fine is that with a rather authentic mood. And what is much more spicy is that with a female lead vocals. Top-notch tunes, kind and versatile arrangements. Great Country Pop.
