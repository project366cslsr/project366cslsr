Awesome combination of classic, traditional and authentic tunes, modern and even a bit psycho moods with clear lightnings of rockin' and rollin'. Furthermore with kind ironic and real tenderness where its needed. "Taggy Tones" EP featured rockin' themes, balladin' stroll, love song and swampy chic.


My experience is based on its Digital release (thanks for this ability to buy it via all these modern channels) and I have to listen to it carefully for proper review.
