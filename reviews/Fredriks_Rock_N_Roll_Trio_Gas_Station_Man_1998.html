Review

028/366 (Project 366)

Raw, fast, eager rockabilly with the first song "Gas Station Man" and a strolling tune with the second song "Too Many", harmonious with a slightly moony tone. Both sounds as if with an authentic style and as if from the revival times. A crucial twist! Because an impeccable approach and its result can be interesting to many. Basic and catchy rhythm pattern, singing and chanting, cool and greasy, lively and ingenuously, captivating and lovely. Another advantage is that the group is from Norway. All in all, a rather interesting single with its own concept and sufficiency. Not sleek, but nifty.
