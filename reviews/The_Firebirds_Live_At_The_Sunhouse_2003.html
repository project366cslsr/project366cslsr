Live album of a quite legendary band. Fantastic!


Starting with classic Pop, fifties-sixties and general vintage mood, normal Doo Wop, they performing lovely Rock'n'Roll then and stream out right to what they, perhaps, proud to be and do - Rockabilly! Rock and Roll, Rockabilly, instrumental composition and love ballads - all is very harmonised. Great tunes, fine sound, nice atmosphere and joyful excitement. Really rockin' and rollin' show.
