Review

143/366 (Project 366)

Pretty interesting, moderately wild and lively smooth Rock'n'Roll. Authentic mood, fresh notes, classic Rockabilly and rockin' roll. Some tracks featuring brass section and piano, maybe this is "Dudebilly" part. However, most of tracks are with drums and guitars only. General sounds again and sounds good! Beautiful vocals, some crazy points and awesome guitar solos. There are some great strollin' tunes, fancy honky tonkin' peppy shake and roll, cruisin' jivers with rockin' and rollin' sparks, wild twistin' boppin' rock and roll, country rockin' and much more. Quite diverse, but very verified in its style. Great guitar sound indeed, fine drums and probably all the very fabulous perks that rockabilly musicians from Finland have. Very enthusiastic and original. With a charming sound of music. Much could be a little better, but I really liked the finished work. Album sounds quite gently and excitingly, with a good contemporary rendition of the old fashioned mood.
