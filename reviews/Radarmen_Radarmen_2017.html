Review

256/366 (Project 366)

Masterpiece modern implementation of the oldschool Psychobilly ideas. When I listened for the first time, it made an incredible impression. So artistic in a sense, how charged, how insane and nutty! Just marvelous and epic.


The beginning with "Invasion Of The Radarmen" is so atmospheric, a surefire hit, especially for live performance. The continuation with "Interstellar" is already in a good way much more surprising. Adventure theme with so groovy and mad arrangement! Finishing the EP with "Cosmic Kidnapping" and "End Of Line" is just awesome! Kind of (neo)Rockabilly with early psychobilly mood. With some mysterious and cosmic speed. Huge power of streaming waves in one rockin' way and another rollin' one.


The whole album is generally saturated with maniac sound and mood. So stylishly frumpy! So psycho!
