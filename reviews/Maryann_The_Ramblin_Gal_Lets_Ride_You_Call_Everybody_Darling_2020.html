299/366 (Project 366)

Authentic vintage with very perky and playful female vocals. Some rockabilly, some rockin' and rollin'. Both tracks are slender and beautiful tunes. Amazing backing musicians, great sound, fabolous mood. Of course "Let's Ride" is a bit more sure thing, very original and so groovy.
