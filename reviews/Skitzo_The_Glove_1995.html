Old school way of Punk'N'Roll, (neo)Rockabilly, mad and crazy Psychobilly. Very original, very enthusiastic, very furious. But somewhat unpolished. And maybe good that it is.


The first track "The Glove" with a stroll that turns into a run, and back then. A kind of rhythm ballad with a blend of pushy psycho groove. The second song "Beatin' My Drum" with energetic sound, power style and stunning guitars. "Killer Love" is a punky (neo)Rockabilly with soft Psychobilly drive. Daring vocals and fruity vibe. And the last "No Return" is actually top hit. Heavy, hard, mighty and mad! So tune with a manic voice, gritty riffs and devastating power. A real punchy end.
