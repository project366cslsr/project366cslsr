Two fine songs, both with pretty Rockabilly Revival and general Country Rock'n'Roll mood.


Energetic rockin' and rollin' with rustic overspeed in "Booger Red". Tune puffs, charmingly and drively. Sounds fancy. And an amazing female Country gentle rock-a-billy in "I Can't Stand The Nights Alone". So sweet, so heartfelt. As it should be. Thoughtfully, dreamily, frankly and mighty.


On the one hand, there is not much special about this single. But if you consider that a rather young teenager is performing, then there is some charm and uprightness in this. Also these songs and artists reflect a rather significant side of the eighties rockin' scene of Finland.
