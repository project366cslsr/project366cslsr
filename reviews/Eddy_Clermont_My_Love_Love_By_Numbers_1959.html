Late fifties authentic mood from Canada. "My Love" is a dreamy rockin' and rollin' vocal song with so popping tune. Great backing, smooth rhythm and all this excellently picking up your sensations! "Love By Numbers" is a fanciful rockin' and rollin' rockabilly number. Raw arrangements, great drums, wonderful backing vocals, sweet voice.
