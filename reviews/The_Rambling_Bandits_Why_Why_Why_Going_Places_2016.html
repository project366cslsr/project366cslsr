Review

103/366 (Project 366)

Wild, wild, wild first track "Why Why Why". Especially with piano and gruff vocals. Rage, chic, crazy a bit but so lovely. So groovy even. Very danceable with sense of a full drive. Catchy chanting is also perky.


Second track "Going Places" with Rockin' and Boppin' start. Very different from the first track, but still with the glitter of rockin' might. Rockabilly chic, raw sound, greasy tunes. Invigorating enough and pretty cheerful, albeit a little crumpled. But with enthusiasm. Indeed fast and loud rockabilly from Denmark.
