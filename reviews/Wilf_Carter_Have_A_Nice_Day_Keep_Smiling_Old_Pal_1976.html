Cheerful Country with sing-a-long and some humorous mood! A somewhat comic and entertaining motive with "Have A Nice Day" and "Keep Smiling Old Pal" - kind of acoustic country folk ballad with kind, friendly and very natural sound. Calm singing and very friendly tune. And another styles of Hillbilly country artists. Wilf Carter (aka Montana Slim) was a very versatile artist and splendidly performed traditional, folk, country, hillbilly and things around it. Even some rockin' and rollin' fun. Usually, filled with optimism, enthusiasm, artistry, drawlingly. Also relatively tragic (but filled with understanding), sensual and soulful lyrics. Hillbilly Country, cheerful, smartly and perky, Playful! As many traditional folk singers did. But Wilf carter - something else and special!
