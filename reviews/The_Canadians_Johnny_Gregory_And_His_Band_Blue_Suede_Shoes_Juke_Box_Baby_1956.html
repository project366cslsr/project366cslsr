The Canadians (also and originally known as The Maple Leaf Four, Canada) with Johnny Gregory and his Band (England, UK). Greatest authentic UK rockin' and rollin' with the performance of the nicest cover songs. Orchestral Rock'n'Roll bandstand music entertainment with a very sleeky sound, groovy, perky, rehearsed, artistic, bright, diverse, smooth and original. Brass instruments created a special festive mood. Not wild, but danceable, graceful and energetic. Made in England and made chicly.
