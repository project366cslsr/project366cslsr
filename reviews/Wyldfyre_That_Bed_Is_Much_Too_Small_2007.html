The modern look of quite authentic but mad Rockin' and Rollin' with touches of modern Rockabilly and Psychobilly. Thus, with high influences from hard and heavy styles. Pretty sound of four tracks which rather single than EP. Three of them are from album "Can You Say Kool?", three are covers. Actually, the album and their own songs sounds a bit more different to covers. Original and furious. However, cover versions are also pretty special.
