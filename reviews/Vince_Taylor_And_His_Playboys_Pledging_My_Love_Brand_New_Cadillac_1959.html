Twistin' ardor of UK Rock'n'Roll, Pop and Rockabilly magicians. Well.. musicians, of course. But musicians with a lot of energy. Unfortunately, it is not always directed in the right aim and is not always demanded at its true worth. These two tracks are nice. "Pledging My Love" is a Rock'n'Roll, fifties Pop love song. And "Brand New Cadillac" is actually much more famous and legendary rockin' and rollin' Rockabilly hit. At least, somewhere and somewhat.
