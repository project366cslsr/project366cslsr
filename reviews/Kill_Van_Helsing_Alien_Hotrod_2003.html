Review

253/366 (Project 366)

Silly (but fine) and uncombed Psychobilly with somewhat freaky lyrics and a highly charged passion. Four live tracks also convey some of the atmosphere of the gigs and contain quite hit things. While the studio stuff is filled with old school. In general, it turns out an album containing some (neo)Rockabilly sides, mood of Punk'n'Roll and cranky Psychobilly elements.
