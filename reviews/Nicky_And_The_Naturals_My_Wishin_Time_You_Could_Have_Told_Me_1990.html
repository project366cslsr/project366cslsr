308/366 (Project 366)

Great rockin' and rollin' Vocal Pop. Sounds so sweet and cute. Remind nice impressions and somewhat groovy'n'beauty. Singalong love songs with Teen Pop style and general vintage mood. Very good tone of backing vocals, drums, overall rhythm and harmonies. "You Could Have Told Me" is a bit more close to special UK way of doing beat, but both tracks are pretty original and stylish. These recordings and the name of the band are the first appearance of the core of future the Roomates.
