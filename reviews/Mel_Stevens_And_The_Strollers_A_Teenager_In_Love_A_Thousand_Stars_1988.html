Review

188/366 (Project 366)

The return of the dreamy sound of the fifties and sixties, and the comeback of the love side of Teen Rock'n'Roll. And certainly nostalgia of the 1980s (with its retrospective backdrop of proportionate sentiments).


The songs sound a bit poppy, have a certain style and are generally gentle. Also shaky a little (it was not for nothing in the style of Shakin' Stevens) and somewhat winsome. Besides, in itself, a very kind atmosphere, a very sweet manner of singing, a charming motive of the songs. They still sound within the genre, not to the media hardline. But they are made in a rather contemporary form and in a brilliant tone. It is also possible to note how nostalgic the guitar sounds. It leaves the feeling that you are alone with that song, although miracles are happening around. If the songs are taken to the point, then this is a great companion for a good evening.


Very famous compositions are performed in a completely unhackneyed form. And even a feeling of tenderness appears and is filled with crisp beauty. Skillful singer with quality accompaniment.
