Wonder fifties rockin' and rollin' rave from UK. Wild not less than Jerry Lee Lewis, hot not less than untamed Rock-A-Billy. And enthusiastically like UK artists can do! It was about the first track "Whole Lotta Shakin' Goin'". The second track "That'll Be The Day" is a stylish cover with a great mood and groove. Very pleasant recordings with a good sense of mood, tone and interesting arrangements. Good covers, great wave!
