Lovely and really great UK Rockabilly Revival with female vocals, a certain vibe, boppin', strollin', rockin' and rollin'! I must say that this is just a masterpiece voice. The manner of performance, general tone, sense of sound - chic! Other than that, some pretty cool guitar solos.


"We Say Yeah!" - dashing start, then vocals and UK beat meet Rock-A-Billy craze. They say yeah - I say yeah too! Sure rave, very revival, so strong. Drums sound marvelously with guitars and this unstoppable lead vocal with so groovy backing is causing such a sensation. The second song "Please Baby, Please" - rather smoothly awakening and nostalgic tune. Rockin' and rollin' with lovely acoustic tone and such a woman voice with youthful passion. Tuny solos, energetic (in a way) rhythm and so catchy chorus. Sweet mood.
